Hello! As the site url implies, my name is Alex Bechanko. I am an enthusiast of mathematics and computer science that resides in an apartment near Ann Arbor Michigan. I studied both topics and obtained a Bachelor’s degree at the Michigan Technological University. In my free time I enjoy playing frisbee golf, keeping up with latest technologies, learning even more math (it never ends), and programming things to apply all I’ve learned constantly.

My main interests and hobbies right now revolve around two topics, the Haskell programming language, and game development. I plan to use this blog mainly as a bounce board for showing the things I’ve learned or have done with these two topics. I find that trying to teach others what I’ve learned helps cement those ideas in my head.
