Updated Look and Structure of Blog

Posted on February 9, 2014

Since the look of the blog was feeling a little stale, I’ve updated the look to something a little simpler but hopefully visually appealing. Along with this update I’ve made changes to the structure of the site. Hopefully they won’t be obvious, but to make things simpler for myself I’ve made the site use HTML5 elements. Most browsers should support what I’ve done, but if there are any problems you have, feel free to let me know and I will try to fix the issue.
