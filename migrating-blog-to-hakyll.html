Migrating Blog to Hakyll

Posted on May 6, 2013

Since I grew tired of working against a few different blogging systems with this blog, I’ve moved to one that is extremely flexible, Hakyll. Since Hakyll allows me to create any kind of structure I want for my site, it was very easy integrating the new parts I wanted into the this system.

I’ve also switched the way I’m hosting the site. Before, I was using a EC2 Ubuntu server instance off of Amazon, but since this blog is completely static, I’ve decided to try deploying an S3 instance instead, and see what benefits or problems I reap.
